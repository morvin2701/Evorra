Frequently Asked Questions

How do I access my booked tickets?

Open "My Tickets" from your profile to view your passes and QR entry details.

What if my QR code does not scan?

Increase screen brightness, make sure the ticket is active, and present the QR clearly at the entry gate.

Can I update my profile information?

Yes, you can edit your name, email, phone number, city, and role from the Edit Profile screen.

How do I switch between attendee and organizer?

Go to Edit Profile and change your role using the role selector section at the bottom.

Important Notes

Always keep your app updated for the best event experience.

Carry a valid ID proof when attending major venue events.

Reach the venue early to avoid delays during entry validation.

Use only official support channels for booking or payment assistance.
